About Tasty Munchies

Tasty Munchies isn't just a restaurant, it's a leafy little dream for hungry souls. Your go-to for planty vibes, late-night cravings, and food that feels like a hug.

Our Origin Story

Tasty Munchies started with a group of friends, cravings and a dream of creating the coziest most unforgettable food spot ever. We wanted to serve amazing food to satisfy the weirdest, wildest, most wonderful cravings. From fried oreos to tryppy lighting and a vibe that makes everyone feel at home. Nairobi's comfiest hangout spot.

What We’re About

  • Cozy, leafy, welcoming spaces
  • Flavor-packed comfort food
  • Chill vibes & community energy
  • Creative, playful presentation

Who We Serve

Students. Artists. You. Anyone craving something tasty, fun, and real. Come as you are, leave full and happy.

From Idea to Reality

2021 – It Began With Cravings

Late-night snacks, weed-fueled group chats, and a craving for something better.

2022 – First Location Opens

We opened our first cozy spot. Neon lights, pillows, playlists, and mad good food.

2024 – Still Growing

More munchies. More memories. Always the same vibes.
